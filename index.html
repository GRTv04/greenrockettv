GreenRocketTV Production

Where Creativity Meets innovation

GreenRocketTV Production is a dynamic and innovative multimedia production house based in Aizawl, Mizoram, North Eastern part of India. Started in 2019 by passionate team of creators, specialize in creating compelling content that resonates with the new generation through photography, videography, art and digital illustrations.

About Us

As a production house, GreenRocketTV is committed to creating high-quality content that not only entertains but also resonates with the local community. With a strong emphasis on innovation and creativity, GreenRocketTV aims to push the boundaries of Mizo cinema and media, exploring new genres and themes that appeal to both local and broader audiences.

Our Services

We bring creativity to life through our diverse range of production services. Our videography expertise covers everything from films, music videos, reels, and short videos to event coverage and advertisements, ensuring each frame tells a compelling story. Our photography services capture life’s most cherished moments—birthdays, weddings, events, magazine shoots, and family portraits—with precision and artistry. We also specialize in art, offering traditional canvas paintings, stunning murals, and digital creations such as posters, flyers, logos, digital illustrations, and animations. Additionally, our audio production services provide professional recording and audio mixing, delivering high-quality sound for any project.Whether through the lens or the brush, we transforms visions into extraordinary visual experiences.
