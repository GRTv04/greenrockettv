Our Story

We started GreenRocketTV (GRTv) in 2019, creating fun YouTube skits with friends. With love and support, we earned our Silver Play Button in 2020 and started a Production House the same year.

We took on both photography and videography for various events. We ventured into art and design, and even started selling our art. Our sound department also grew, offering recording and mixing services. We believe in collaboration, supporting startups and collaborating with big brands. A major milestone came in 2023, when we made our first feature film, "Rawng Chawhpawlh", a cult classic reflecting society and lifestyle.

It was selected by various film festivals, marking a proud moment for us.
Today, our focus is on advertisement videos, event coverage, photography, filmmaking, and art—continuing our journey of creativity and storytelling.
